HOW TO TURN YOUR TRENDS
INTO TREASURES


AND YOUR CLOSET INTO CASH $$$




By: Fashionista Diva

STEP: 1 MINDSET
It's about "Out with the old and in with the new". Clear those chakras and negative energy. Try your hand at minimalism or even just live joyfully in the Mari Kondo way. You won't be albe to receive or make room for the new with all the "old handbags" around.

Try One of Fashionista Diva's Offer Up Classes
STEP: 2 STEP 2: CLEAN & STEAM
After sorting everything out, take this time to spot clean, dry clean, or steam any garments or items that need a little polish. Take pride in what you give to others. It’s good karma and leaves a lasting impression. You'll also be surprised by the good reviews you’ll get.

Try One of Fashionista Diva's Old Fashioned Yard Sale Classes
STEP: 3 : PHOTOS & LIGHTING
Try to invest a little money in acquiring mannequins and adequate lighting. If you take the time to set up really nice shots, ones that the clients can imagine themselves in your clothing...it betters your chances at making sales. Even if you can't spend a lot. Simply taking the time to set up a proper photo will take you far!

Try One of Fashionista Diva's Tradesy Classes
STEP: 4 PRICING & POSTING
This can sometimes be the difficult part because you have to take into consideration 1) people who will ask for a discount or bargain, and rightfully so. 2) The cost of shipping which is $7.11 at this time on Poshmark per person you shop with. And, 3) Don't forget to account for the time it will take you to wrap and ship it, standing in line at the post office, and cost of cards.

Try One of Fashionista Diva's The Real Real Classes
STEP: 5 SELL & SHIP
Voila! You've done the work. It's time to reap the rewards. Once an item is purchased, make sure to get it to the post office quickly so that they can say it arrived on time, if not early. That always brightens some one’s day. Also, don't forget to include a fun, lovely, or charming little card or extra, small gift, just a token of appreciation, as I call it, for making their purchase. Going that extra mile makes you memorable and they may shop with you again!
